How to play ?

  1. First click "Roll dice" button to play.
  2. The dice no is added in current score until you got dice no 1.
  3. If you get 1, the player is switched.
  4. To add the current score to final score, click "Hold" button.
  5. If you click "Hold", the current score is added to final score and the player is switched.
  6. The player who scores 30 first wins.
